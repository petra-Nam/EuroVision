Welcome to the Eurovision Voting Portal

Please read the voting regulations carefully before submitting your scores.

General Public

As a member of the global audience, your voice decides who stays and who goes! In the Semi-Finals, the result is 100% in your hands. You may vote for any song except the one representing your own country.

  • Multiple Votes: You can award points to multiple different songs.
  • Selection: Use the dropdown to choose your favorite and click 'Vote'.

National Jury

Professional Juries evaluate the Grand Final based on vocal capacity, performance, and composition. Juries must provide a full ranking of their top songs.

  • The Unique Score Rule: You cannot award the same point value (e.g., 12 points) to two different countries.
  • Point Scale: You must distribute exactly 1-8, 10, and 12 points across your top 10 favorites.
